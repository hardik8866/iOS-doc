Fiy it by adding an explicit cast to “long”:
(your original code):
self.screenNumber.text = [NSString stringWithFormat:@”Screen #%d”, self.index];
(then will become…)

self.screenNumber.text = [NSString stringWithFormat:@”Screen #%ld”, (long)self.index];

For those below having problems then subclassing the view in Storyboard, after creating a UITableViewController and connecting it to the view, go in and either delete or comment out the template code, which is actually for dynamic cells. In particular, blocking out the following methods will prevent the blank-screen thing, and will also allow you to hook up outlets as usual to your .h file.
/*
– (NSInteger)numberOfSectionsInTableView:(UITableView *)tableView
– (NSInteger)tableView:(UITableView *)tableView numberOfRowsInSection:(NSInteger)section
*/

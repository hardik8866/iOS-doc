The idea is to have the constraint of 0 distance from your button’s left edge to the container’s left edge, and 0 distance from the button’s right edge to the container’s right edge. What I did at first was, since I didn’t resize the button, and it was 200 pixels away from the left edge, I unwittingly asked Autolayout to enforce that distance between the button and the container edge – and this pushed the button outside the screen.

Build an App without Coding

Simply plug your own RSS feeds and turn the Xcode template into a Blog Reader App

  • Select Your RSS Feeds

    With the built-in RSS parser, you just need to add your own RSS feeds in a configuration file and can easily turn the template into a blog reader app.


  • Choose Your Own Theme

    Two beautiful themes included (more are coming). You just need to set a parameter in the configuration file to choose your preferred theme. With a few tweaks, you'll have an app with your own design.


  • Create Your Own App

    The app template is completely written in Swift. It supports Xcode 6.3 and iOS 8 (or up). Once you complete your configuration, hit the Build button to create your own app.


Save You Thousands of Dollars

To build a similar app like this, it will cost you over $2000 to hire an iOS developer. Even if you know Swift or Objective-C, the app template will save you 100+ hours from building the app from scratch.

  • Built-in Dropdown and Sidebar Menus

    We have created two menus for you and made it easy for your customisations. If you don't want the menus, simply change a setting of the configuration file to disable them.

  • Designed for iPhone and iPad

    Built as a universal app, the app template supports all iOS devices including iPhone 6 and 6 Plus. The UI design is specially crafted to take advantage of the iPad's screen. If your feeds have images, they will be beautifully displayed. 

  • Ad Ready and Social Sharing Built-in

    The template is integrated with iAd. When you're ready to monetise your app, simply enable an option to display a banner ad. Even more, your users can share articles on Facebook and Twitter using the built-in social sharing feature.

  • Multiple App UI Designs

    The template includes multiple storyboards for you to choose from. Simply pick the one you love, build the project and you'll achieve a different app UI.


  • Customise It Your Way

    We know you want your app to look distinct. The template is built with this in mind. You can easily customise the template by using a XML configuration file. Just change a setting to switch theme or enable ads. Of course, you're free to modify the source code to further tweak the app and extend its features.
